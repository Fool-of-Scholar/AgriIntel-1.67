# AgriIntel Demo Video Script

**Total Duration: 4 minutes 30 seconds**

---

## SCENE 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:45)

### Visual
- Open with dashboard view of AgriIntel
- Slow pan across the interface showing stats and recent activity

### Narration

> "Every year, Filipino farmers lose millions of pesos due to inconsistent crop grading. Without standardized references, a farmer might grade their mangoes as premium quality, only to have them rejected at the export facility. This disconnect leads to wasted produce, lost income, and broken trust in the supply chain."

> "AgriIntel solves this problem. It's a smart crop grading and quality assurance platform that gives every farmer access to standardized grading tools, digital traceability, and expert verification - all from their smartphone."

---

## SCENE 2: The Problem Deep Dive (0:45 - 1:15)

### Visual
- Show statistics cards on dashboard
- Highlight the gap between "Total Graded" and "Verified" counts

### Narration

> "The agricultural industry faces four critical challenges. First, inconsistent grading - farmers lack visual references to accurately assess their crops. Second, no traceability - there's no digital record linking produce from farm to market. Third, limited expert access - agricultural specialists are scarce in rural areas. And fourth, weather impact blindness - farmers can't correlate environmental conditions with quality outcomes."

> "AgriIntel addresses all four with an integrated platform designed for field use."

---

## SCENE 3: Feature Demo - Visual Validator (1:15 - 2:15)

### Visual
- Navigate to `/grade` page
- Click "Start New Grading"
- Demonstrate photo upload
- Show GPS location capture
- Use the comparison slider to compare uploaded photo against Grade A-E references

### Narration

> "Let me show you how it works. On the grading page, a farmer starts by uploading a photo of their crop. They can take a picture directly with their phone camera or select from their gallery."

> "Notice that AgriIntel automatically captures the GPS coordinates and converts them to a readable location name. This creates an immutable record of where the produce was harvested."

> "Now here's the core feature - the Visual Validator. Using this side-by-side comparison slider, farmers can drag to compare their actual crop against standardized grade references, from Grade A Premium Export quality down to Grade E Reject."

> "Each grade has clear descriptions based on Philippine export standards. Grade A means perfect condition with no blemishes. Grade B has minor superficial marks but is still export quality. Grade C is suitable for domestic markets only. And so on."

> "The farmer selects the grade that best matches their crop, and the system generates actionable insights."

---

## SCENE 4: Feature Demo - Smart Coach (2:15 - 2:50)

### Visual
- Show Smart Coach panel with weather data and insights
- Highlight specific IF/THEN recommendations
- Show the weather chart visualization

### Narration

> "The Smart Coach engine is where AgriIntel becomes truly intelligent. It automatically fetches 7-day weather data including rainfall, wind speed, temperature, and humidity. It also analyzes soil conditions like pH levels and moisture content."

> "Using IF/THEN logic, it generates contextual recommendations. For example, if a farmer grades their crop as C after heavy rainfall, the system suggests reinforcing transport padding because the fruit may have softened. High wind conditions trigger warnings to check for mechanical damage on the windward side."

> "These insights help farmers make better post-harvest decisions."

---

## SCENE 5: Feature Demo - Digital Passport (2:50 - 3:25)

### Visual
- Complete grading to generate a passport
- Navigate to `/passports` page
- Show passport details with Batch ID
- Demonstrate the verification status indicator

### Narration

> "Once grading is complete, AgriIntel generates a Digital Passport - a unique, tamper-evident record of the harvest. Each passport has a Batch ID in the format AGR followed by a timestamp and random string, making it globally unique."

> "The passport binds together the farmer's selected grade, the high-resolution photo, GPS-tagged location, timestamp, weather conditions at harvest, and Smart Coach insights. This creates complete traceability from field to market."

> "On the Passports page, farmers can view all their records, filter by grade or verification status, and share passport details with buyers or inspectors."

---

## SCENE 6: Feature Demo - Expert Verification (3:25 - 4:00)

### Visual
- Navigate to `/expert` page
- Show expert registration form
- Switch to Expert Dashboard tab
- Demonstrate verification queue and ground truth assignment

### Narration

> "The final piece is the Expert Verification Portal. Agricultural professionals - certified agronomists, researchers, cooperative leads, and agriculture students - can register with their credentials for verification."

> "Once verified, experts access a dashboard showing a queue of farmer-submitted photos awaiting verification. They can view the farmer's self-assigned grade, examine the photo in detail, and assign what we call the Ground Truth grade - the expert's professional assessment."

> "This creates a feedback loop. Farmers learn from expert corrections, and the system builds a valuable training dataset for future machine learning integration."

---

## SCENE 7: Technologies & Closing (4:00 - 4:30)

### Visual
- Return to dashboard
- Show responsive design by resizing browser
- End with AgriIntel logo/branding

### Narration

> "AgriIntel is built with modern web technologies. Next.js 16 and React 19 power the frontend. Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui provide the accessible, mobile-first interface. TypeScript ensures type safety, while Recharts handles data visualization. The entire application was developed using v0 by Vercel, an AI-powered development assistant."

> "AgriIntel transforms how farmers approach quality control. With standardized visual grading, digital traceability, intelligent coaching, and expert verification, we're building trust and transparency into agricultural supply chains - one harvest at a time."

> "Thank you for watching."

## Technical Notes for Recording

### Screen Recording Setup
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Browser: Chrome or Firefox (latest)
- Clear browser cache before recording
- Use a clean browser profile without extensions

### Demonstration Checklist
- [ ] Dashboard loads with statistics
- [ ] Navigate to Grade page
- [ ] Upload a sample crop photo
- [ ] GPS location captures successfully
- [ ] Comparison slider works smoothly
- [ ] Select a grade (recommend Grade C for best insight demo)
- [ ] Smart Coach displays weather data and insights
- [ ] Passport generates with unique Batch ID
- [ ] Passports page shows records
- [ ] Expert registration form displays
- [ ] Expert dashboard shows verification queue

### Voiceover Tips
- Speak clearly at moderate pace
- Pause briefly when demonstrating interactions
- Match narration timing to on-screen actions
- Total word count: ~750 words at ~170 words/minute = ~4.5 minutes
